Thank You For Ordering With Paper Masters!

What do I do next?

  1. Check your e-mail for an automated confirmation of your order.
  2. If you are a new customer, you will receive a phone call from one of our representatives within the next 12 to 24 hours to confirm your order.
  3. Once we have a writer scheduled for your project and we have called you, we will send you another confirmation of the charge that has taken place.
  4. Your paper will arrive in your e-mail in the evening of the date that you ordered it for.

What you need to know:

  • If you do not receive confirmation of your order in e-mail or by telephone within 12 hours, please call us at 1-570-955-1438.
  • Standard procedure for new customers is that a Paper Masters representative will contact you shortly via the telephone to verify your order.
  • Our valued, repeat customers will simply receive a confirmation of their order in e-mail.
  • All charges go on your credit card statement as "E World Publishing, Inc.". E World Publishing, Inc. is the parent company of Paper Masters.
  • If for any reason you do not receive your project by 11:59pm on the date you ordered it for, call us immediately at 1-570-955-1438.

Your Project Format

Please note that all projects are done in the following format:

  • Written in 12 pt. Courier New Font
  • Contain 1" margins on all sides

  • Are written in MLA format unless otherwise specified
