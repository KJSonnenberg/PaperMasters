Contact Paper Masters

Paper Masters was started in 1998 in San Diego, CA in a 1 room office. Recognizing the need for a research company that did only custom written term papers and reports, the small staff began hiring freelance writers to produce custom research. By the year 2000, Paper Masters needed to branch out and was acquired by E World Publishing, Inc. and moved to beautiful Northeastern Pennsylvania.

Paper Masters - Growing to Meet Your Custom Writing Needs

Today, Paper Masters has 2 satellite locations and a full staff to accommodate the over 12,000 custom research paper orders we receive a year. Under E World's outstanding commitment to academic research, Paper Masters experienced a boom in growth.

Paper Masters has many writers in virtually every field of study, a full staff and yet still holds on to its original mission - providing quality, custom research with outstanding attention to customer service.

E World Publishing, Inc. is dedicated to providing outstanding customer service 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. With friendly staff members always available for your questions, you never have to worry if you've made the right choice for your research needs.
